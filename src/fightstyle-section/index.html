Unarmed Fighting Style

Unarmed fighting style gives the user an understanding of how to make his fist/body part strike hard, fast and with proper technique and how to move his body according to that understanding.

Characters

Monkey D. Luffy, Boa Hancock, Nico Robin, Tony Tony Chopper, Charlotte Katakuri, Marshal D. Teach, Sanji, Monkey D. Garp, Cobby, Roby Lucci, etc.

Armed Fighting Style

Armed Fighting Style gives the user an understanding of how to use any type of weapon thus making him a weapons master. Within this category, there are several other styles that, mastered, can make you the strongest in combat, making your enemies fear you just by hearing your name. In the One Piece universe, the sword style is the most common.

Characters

Roronoa Zoro, Dracule Mihawk, Edward Newgate, Shanks, Hacchi, Fujitora, Trafalgar D. Walter Law, Silvers Rayleigh, Gol D. Roger, etc.

Ranged Fighting Style

Ranged Fighting Style gives the user the ability to attack from a distance, allowing him to have good precision skills and use any type of ranged weapon, giving him an advantage in combat.

Characters

Usopp, Nami, Yasopp, Lucky Roo, Aokiji, Akainu, Kizaru, Benn Beckman, Izou, Capone Bege, Van Augur, etc.

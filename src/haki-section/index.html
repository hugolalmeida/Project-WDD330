Haki

"'Haki' is a power that lies dormant in all the world's creatures... "Presence", "fighting spirit" and "intimidation"... It is not different from the things that humans can naturally sense such as these... 'The act of not doubting'. That is strength!"

Kenbunshoku Haki

Kenbunshoku Haki is a form of Haki that allows the user to sense the presence of others, even if they are hidden or too far away to be seen naturally. If trained enough, the user can see a little bit into the future.
There are three categories: Basic: The most common use of this Haki allows the user to sense the presence of others. Intermediate: The user can also use this Haki to predict an opponent's moves just before making them. Advanced: Predict future movements whether there are murderous intentions in the environment or not. The user can see a little bit into the future.

Haoshoku Haki

Haoshoku Haki is a rare form of Haki, only one in several million people has this ability. This type of Haki allows the user to exert their willpower on others. It is said that whoever possesses this type of Haki has the qualities of a king.
There are three categories: Basic: The most common usage is to exert the user's willpower on those with weak desires and render them unconscious. Intermediate: The user can pick weak-willed individuals out of a large group and eliminate them without affect others Advanced:: The users are capable of damaging inanimate objects or structures around them and users are able to infuse the body and weapons with the spirit of the conqueror in a very similar way to Busoshoku Haki , greatly increasing the power of attacks.

Busoshoku Haki

Busoshoku Haki is a form of Haki that allows the user to use their spirit to create, in essence, invisible armor around themselves, providing potentially incredible offensive and defensive capabilities. Notably, this form of Haki can be used to injure Devil Fruit users who are immune to certain types of physical damage, most notably Logia users.
There are three categories: Basic: Grants a power and resistance enhancement. It can be used on a single body part or on the user's entire body. Intermediate: A strong concentration of this Haki can harden the user's body, turning the user's body a shiny black. The strengthening effect of this technique is able to easily block sword attacks and even break them. By touching an object, the user can imbue it with their own Haki to greatly increase their power and endurance. Advanced: The user can project their Haki flow a short distance from their body without a medium. This method allows the user to damage the target without making contact, but they still need to remain relatively close.
